Account Management Instructions

Follow these instructions to Request for deletion of your Ngalo App account.

Delete Account Instructions:

To request your account to be deleted, please use the "Request Delete Account" button in your app's settings. This will guide you through the account deletion process.

  1. Click the Delete Account button/text in the settings screen
  2. You will be asked to confirm whether you are sure you want to delete the account. This is to make sure you are not mistakenly deleting your account
  3. Once you confirm the deletion the request to delete your account will be sent and the account will be deactivated for a mean time as it awaits deletion, unless you signup again you will not be able to access you account again. The account will be deleted with in 90 days. Thank you for supporting Ngalo Cycling Services
