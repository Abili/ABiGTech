Account Management Instructions

Follow these instructions to manage your Ngalo App account.

Delete Account Instructions:

To delete your account, please use the "Delete Account" button in your app's settings. This will guide you through the account deletion process.

Please note that the delete account button is initially not visible until you read these instructions, now when you go back to the app the button will be visible.

Contact Support:

If you encounter any issues or need assistance, you can contact our support team by sending an email to abiliisaac@gmail.com. Please provide relevant details for a quicker response.
